Spliiit makes splitting expenses with friends and groups effortless.

Whether you're sharing rent, splitting a dinner bill, tracking road trip costs, or managing household expenses, Spliiit keeps everyone on the same page.

KEY FEATURES:

Groups & Friends
Create groups for trips, households, or events. Add friends for 1-on-1 expense tracking. Invite anyone via email.

Smart Expense Splitting
Split equally or with custom amounts. Track who paid what and who owes whom. Simplified debt calculations minimize the number of payments needed.

Settle Up
See exactly what you owe and what you're owed. Settle up with one tap. Keep a clear record of all settlements.

Import from Splitwise
Moving from Splitwise? Import your CSV export directly. All members, expenses, and balances transfer seamlessly.

Receipt Scanner
Snap a photo of your receipt to attach it to any expense. Keep a visual record of what you spent.

Export Your Data
Export expenses as CSV via email. Works with Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet app.

Email Notifications
Get notified when someone adds an expense involving you, invites you to a group, or settles up.

Privacy First
Your data stays yours. We don't sell or share your information. Passwords are securely hashed. All connections are encrypted.
